Welcome to the GCE Cultural Club!

We celebrate the vibrant culture and diversity of Gaya College of Engineering through various artistic performances and cultural activities.

About Us

The GCE Cultural Club showcases and promotes various art forms including music, dance, drama, and more. We organize cultural events, competitions, and workshops to encourage students to express their creativity and appreciate different art forms.

Join our club to unleash your artistic talents, participate in captivating performances, and experience the richness of culture at GCE.
