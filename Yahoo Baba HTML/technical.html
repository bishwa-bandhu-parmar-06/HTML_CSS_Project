Welcome to the GCE Technical Club!

We are a group of technology enthusiasts at Gaya College of Engineering, passionate about exploring and expanding our knowledge in the field of technology.

About Us

The GCE Technical Club aims to provide a platform for students to learn, collaborate, and work on technical projects. We organize workshops, hackathons, and technical events to foster learning and innovation among the members.

Join our club to enhance your technical skills, network with like-minded individuals, and stay updated with the latest advancements in technology.
