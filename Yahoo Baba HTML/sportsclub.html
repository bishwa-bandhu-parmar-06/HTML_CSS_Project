Welcome to the GCE Sports Club!

We are dedicated to promoting physical fitness, teamwork, and a passion for sports among the students of Gaya College of Engineering.

About Us

The GCE Sports Club offers a wide range of sports activities and organizes various tournaments and events throughout the year. Our goal is to provide a platform for students to engage in healthy competition, develop their athletic skills, and foster a sense of camaraderie among the GCE community.

Whether you're a seasoned athlete or new to sports, we welcome everyone to join our club and participate in the sports of their choice. We have dedicated coaches and training facilities for different sports, ensuring that each member receives proper guidance and support.
